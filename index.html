Pokemon Gym Battle Game!

HOW TO PLAY

  • There are four gym badges under these instructions.
  • You will be shown a random number (start score)
  • When you click on a gym badge, your Total Score will increase.
  • If your Total Score matches your Start Score, you win!
  • If your Total Score goes above the Start Score, you lose.
